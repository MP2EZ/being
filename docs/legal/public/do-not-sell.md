# Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** December 12, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

---

## Being Does Not Sell Your Personal Information

**We want to be clear: Being does not sell your personal information. Period.**

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), "sale" of personal information means sharing it with third parties in exchange for money or other valuable consideration.

---

## Our Commitment

### We Do NOT:

- Sell your personal information to data brokers
- Share your data with advertisers
- Exchange your information for monetary compensation
- Provide your data to third parties for their marketing purposes
- Share your mental health data with insurance companies
- Sell your wellness assessment results

### We Do NOT Share for Cross-Context Behavioral Advertising:

- We do not share your information for targeted advertising
- We do not participate in advertising networks
- We do not allow third-party tracking on our Services

---

## Why We Collect Data

The only reasons we collect your personal information:

1. **To Provide Our Services:** Enable check-ins, assessments, and progress tracking
2. **To Protect Your Safety:** Detect crisis indicators and offer resources
3. **To Improve the App:** Analyze anonymized usage patterns
4. **To Provide Support:** Help you when you contact us

---

## Third Parties We Work With

We use limited third-party service providers to operate Being:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Cloud infrastructure (optional backup) | Encrypted data you choose to backup |
| **Expo** | App updates and crash reporting | Anonymized crash reports only |
| **Apple/Google** | App distribution and payments | Subscription status (no health data) |

These providers:
- Are contractually prohibited from selling your data
- Can only use data to provide services to us
- Have no access to your decrypted health information

## Your Rights

Even though we don't sell your data, you still have rights:

### Opt-Out Request

If you want to formally exercise your right to opt out of sale/sharing:

**Email:** [privacy@being.fyi](mailto:privacy@being.fyi)

**Subject line:** "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"

We will confirm receipt and document your request, even though no sale is occurring.

### Global Privacy Control

Being honors Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. If your browser or device sends a GPC signal, we will treat it as an opt-out request.

### Other Privacy Rights

See our [California Privacy Rights](/privacy/california) page for additional rights including:
- Right to know what data we collect
- Right to delete your data
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to non-discrimination

This protects you from unauthorized requests.
