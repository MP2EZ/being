# Notice of Privacy Practices

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** December 12, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

## About This Notice

This Notice of Privacy Practices describes how Being collects, uses, and protects your wellness and mental health information. We are committed to maintaining the privacy of your personal information.

> **Important:** Being is a wellness application, not a healthcare provider. We are not a HIPAA-covered entity. However, we voluntarily adopt strong privacy practices to protect your sensitive mental health data.

---

## Information We Collect

### Wellness Assessment Data

Being collects responses from standardized wellness screening tools:

**PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9)**
- Your responses to 9 wellness screening questions
- Calculated scores for self-monitoring
- Assessment completion timestamps
- **Purpose:** Personal tracking and awareness of mood patterns

**GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7)**
- Your responses to 7 wellness screening questions
- Calculated scores for self-monitoring
- Assessment completion timestamps
- **Purpose:** Personal tracking and awareness of stress/anxiety patterns

### Daily Check-In Data

- Morning, midday, and evening mood check-ins
- Reflection entries and journal notes
- Progress through mindfulness exercises
- **Purpose:** Supporting your mindfulness practice and self-awareness

### Crisis Safety Information

- Personal warning signs you identify
- Coping strategies that work for you
- Emergency contact information (stored locally only)
- **Purpose:** Immediate access during difficult moments

---

## How We Use Your Information

Your wellness data is used to:

1. **Provide Core Services:** Enable check-ins, assessments, and progress tracking
2. **Support Your Safety:** Detect when scores indicate you may benefit from crisis resources
3. **Personalize Your Experience:** Adapt content based on your preferences
4. **Improve the App:** Analyze anonymized patterns to enhance features

We **DO NOT** use your data for:
- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- Training AI models
- Insurance or employment decisions

---

## How We Protect Your Information

### Encryption

- **At Rest:** AES-256 encryption on your device
- **In Transit:** TLS 1.2+ encryption for any network communication

### Local-First Storage

Your wellness data is stored on your device by default. Cloud backup is optional and requires your explicit consent.

### Access Controls

- Only you can access your data
- We cannot decrypt your information
- No employee access to your personal wellness data

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
- View all wellness data stored in the app
- Export your complete history in JSON format

### Correct Your Data
- Edit or update your information
- Modify past entries (with change tracking)

### Delete Your Data
- Delete specific entries or assessments
- Clear all data while keeping app functionality
- Request complete account deletion

### Control Your Data
- Enable or disable cloud backup
- Choose what data to sync
- Opt out of anonymized analytics

---

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Local wellness data | Until you delete it |
| Cloud backup (if enabled) | Until you disable or delete account |
| Account deletion request | Processed within 30 days |

---

## Sharing Your Information

We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent:** When you choose to export and share with a healthcare provider
2. **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or court order
3. **Safety Emergencies:** If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm

We **NEVER** share your data with:
- Advertisers or data brokers
- Insurance companies
- Employers
- Social media platforms

---

## How to Exercise Your Rights

**In-App:** Use Settings > Privacy to access, export, or delete your data

**By Email:** Contact [privacy@being.fyi](mailto:privacy@being.fyi) with your request

**Response Time:** We will respond to requests within 30 days

## Complaints

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may:

1. Contact us at [privacy@being.fyi](mailto:privacy@being.fyi)
2. File a complaint with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC)
3. Contact your state attorney general's office
