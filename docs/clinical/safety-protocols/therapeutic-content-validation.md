## üéØ PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### Validation Objectives
This protocol establishes comprehensive procedures for validating all therapeutic content within the FullMind MBCT application to ensure:
- **Clinical Accuracy**: All content aligns with evidence-based MBCT practices
- **User Safety**: No content poses risk of psychological harm or triggers
- **Therapeutic Effectiveness**: Content supports positive mental health outcomes
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Inclusive and appropriate for diverse user populations
- **Professional Standards**: Meets or exceeds clinical practice guidelines

### Content Categories Covered
This protocol applies to all therapeutic content including:
- Assessment questions and scoring algorithms (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
- Mindfulness exercise scripts and guided meditations
- Check-in flow questions and response prompts
- Crisis intervention messaging and resource information
- Educational content about depression, anxiety, and MBCT
- User interface text with therapeutic implications
- Progress tracking and feedback messages

### Regulatory Framework
All validation procedures align with:
- Evidence-based clinical practice standards
- Digital mental health application guidelines
- Crisis intervention best practices
- Cultural competency requirements
- Trauma-informed care principles
