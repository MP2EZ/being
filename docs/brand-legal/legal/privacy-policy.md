# FullMind Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated**: January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

FullMind is a mental health and mindfulness application designed to support your wellbeing through Stoic Mindfulness practices (including virtue development, gratitude exercises, and philosophical reflection), mood tracking, and mental health assessments. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal health information with the highest level of security and care.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FullMind mobile application ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

**Key Privacy Principles**:
- **Local-First**: Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose otherwise
- **Minimal Collection**: We only collect data necessary for therapeutic functionality
- **User Control**: You maintain complete control over your personal data
- **Transparency**: Clear explanation of all data practices
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade encryption and protection measures

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Mental Health Assessment Data

We collect responses and scores from standardized mental health assessments:

**PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9)**:
- Your responses to 9 depression screening questions
- Calculated depression severity scores
- Assessment completion timestamps
- **Clinical Purpose**: Evidence-based depression screening and monitoring

**GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7)**:
- Your responses to 7 anxiety screening questions
- Calculated anxiety severity scores
- Assessment completion timestamps
- **Clinical Purpose**: Evidence-based anxiety screening and monitoring

**Data Storage**: All assessment data is encrypted and stored locally on your device using AES-256 encryption. Assessment scores are used solely for providing you with appropriate therapeutic content and crisis intervention when necessary.

### 2.2 Daily Mood and Check-In Data

**Mood Tracking Information**:
- Daily mood ratings and descriptive selections
- Check-in responses about your mental state
- Reflection entries (optional text input)
- Timestamps for therapeutic progress tracking

**Stoic Mindfulness Practice Data**:
- Virtue practice tracking (instances of wisdom, courage, justice, temperance)
- Personal reflections on virtue challenges and growth
- Gratitude exercises and philosophical reflection entries
- Stoic principle application in daily life (dichotomy of control, premeditatio malorum)
- Morning/midday/evening practice session completion
- Progress through Stoic educational content
- **Purpose**: Supporting your philosophical practice and personal development

### 2.3 Crisis Safety Information

**Safety Planning Data**:
- Personal warning signs you identify
- Coping strategies that work for you
- Emergency contact information (names and phone numbers)
- Safety plan details you create
- **Purpose**: Immediate access during mental health crises

**Crisis Detection Data**:
- Automatic crisis threshold triggers (PHQ-9 ≥20, GAD-7 ≥15)
- Crisis button interaction logs
- Emergency protocol activation records
- **Purpose**: Ensuring your safety during mental health emergencies

### 2.4 App Usage and Technical Data

**Local Usage Analytics**:
- Feature usage patterns (which sections you use most)
- Session duration and frequency
- Navigation patterns within the app
- **Note**: This data is anonymized and used solely for improving therapeutic effectiveness

**Technical Information**:
- Device type and operating system version
- App version and configuration settings
- Error logs and crash reports (no personal data included)
- **Purpose**: App stability and performance optimization

### 2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not access your camera, microphone, or photos
- We do not collect your location data
- We do not access your contacts (except emergency contacts you manually enter)
- We do not track your activity outside the FullMind app
- We do not collect demographic information unless you voluntarily provide it

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Device Storage

**Primary Storage Location**: All your personal health information is stored locally on your device using iOS/Android secure storage systems.

**Encryption Standards**:
- **AES-256 encryption** for all sensitive mental health data
- **Device-level encryption** leveraging iOS Keychain/Android Keystore
- **Secure key management** with biometric protection where available

**Access Controls**:
- Biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) when available
- Device passcode fallback protection
- Automatic app locking after inactivity
- Session timeout for additional security

### 4.2 No Network Transmission (Phase 1)

**Current Privacy Protection**:
- **No cloud storage** of personal health information
- **No data transmission** to external servers
- **No third-party data sharing** with analytics or advertising platforms
- **Complete offline functionality** for all therapeutic features

**Anonymous Usage Only**:
- Crash reporting (no personal data included)
- Feature usage statistics (anonymized and aggregated)
- Performance metrics (no identification possible)

### 4.3 Future Network Features (Your Choice)

**Optional Cloud Sync** (Planned for future release):
- **Explicit opt-in required** - never automatic
- **End-to-end encryption** with client-side key generation
- **Zero-knowledge architecture** - we cannot access your decrypted data
- **Granular control** over what data, if any, you choose to sync
