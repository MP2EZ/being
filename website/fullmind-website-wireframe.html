Privacy Questions

Everything you need to know about your data

What happens to my data if I delete the app?
â–¼

All your data is permanently deleted from your device when you uninstall Being. Since we don't store your personal data on our servers, there's nothing to delete on our end. Your data exists only on your device.

Can my therapist or doctor access my Being. data?
â–¼

Only if you choose to share it. You can export reports and progress summaries to share with your healthcare providers, but they have no direct access to your Being. data. All sharing is manual and under your complete control.

How does Being. make money if it's not selling my data?
â–¼

Being. uses a simple subscription model. We make money from monthly and annual subscriptions, not from your data. This aligns our business incentives with your privacy and creates a sustainable model for ongoing development.

What anonymous data does Being. collect?
â–¼

Only basic usage patterns (like which features are used most often) and crash reports to improve app stability. This data is completely anonymous - we can't trace it back to you. You can opt out of this collection entirely in Privacy Settings.

Is Being. truly HIPAA compliant?
â–¼

Being. is designed to meet HIPAA standards when used in healthcare settings. Our architecture, encryption, and privacy controls support HIPAA compliance, but full compliance also depends on how healthcare providers implement and use the app.

What happens to my data if Being. shuts down?
â–¼

Since your data lives on your device, Being. shutting down wouldn't affect your existing data. We'd provide at least 90 days notice and tools to export your complete history. Your data remains yours, always.
