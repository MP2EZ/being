## üåê Website Overview

The **Being website** serves as the primary marketing and information hub for the Being MBCT app. It provides:

- **Product information** and feature explanations
- **Clinical backing** and MBCT methodology details
- **Download links** and app store redirects
- **Support resources** and FAQ
- **Privacy policy** and terms of service

## üéØ Key Features

### Accessibility First
- **WCAG AA compliance** across all pages
- **Screen reader optimization** for inclusive access
- **High contrast themes** and dark mode support
- **Keyboard navigation** and focus management

### Performance Optimized
- **Fast loading times** for immediate access
- **Mobile-first responsive** design
- **Optimized assets** and efficient bundling
- **Core Web Vitals** performance targets

### Clinical Context
- **Accurate MBCT information** aligned with app content
- **Therapeutic language** consistent with clinical standards
- **Crisis resources** and emergency contact information
- **Evidence-based content** supporting app claims

## üö® Content Guidelines

### Clinical Accuracy
- All therapeutic content must align with app clinical standards
- MBCT methodology descriptions must be clinically accurate
- Mental health information must be evidence-based
- Crisis resources must be current and appropriate

### Brand Consistency
- Visual design must match app branding
- Tone and messaging must align with therapeutic approach
- "Being." branding (with period) used consistently
- Clinical disclaimers included where appropriate
